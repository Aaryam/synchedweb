Synched Clock

Synched Clock is an app, made by Synched for the Flutter Clock competition. It is an app which one can view their own time. As well as several other popular timezones, with more to come. We plan on implementing several other features to the app, and have begun already with the development of the stopwatch. We hope to make a quality and usable app.
